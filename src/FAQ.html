FAQ

Q

Who is 1salerx?

A

1salerx.com is owned and operated by Helios Pharmacy a licensed Texas Board of Pharmacy entity.

Q

What do you offer?

A

1SaleRx offers coupons on weekly basis for brand medications at no cost. Coupons are free and branded medications are free on our site. This is not a Joke!

Q

Why branded medications are free?

A

Yes, free! 1salerx, over the years, has made contacts in the wholesale and retail medication industry, over-stock medication buyers, mail-order companies and many others in the health care business. Our network allows us to bring you weekly deals on prescription medications.

Q

Do 1SaleRx Coupons Expire?

A

Yes. Coupons expire after 30 days from the time you received it in your email.

Q

How does 1SaleRx make money?

A

We promote our network vendors and the same vendors pay us for every potential customer we refer, simplified marketing.

Q

Can you tell more about the process?

A

You just need to follow next few steps:


  1. You select the weekly medication product you want;

  2. Fill in your Name, Email and the phone number;

  3. Our representative will call you to verify your inquiry;

  4. Upload or email your medication prescription from your doctor;

  5. Pay for express shipping for the medications, depending on location the shipping is up to $80 in total for any medications.
